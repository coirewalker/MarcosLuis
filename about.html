Born in Nashville, TN but raised in Pittsburgh, PA , Marcos got his start in the entertainment industry by appearing in stage plays, and a Host of "Kidsburgh" and having since graduated to television and film. Since his introduction as an actor he has added singer, musician, and Award winning Host ,photographer, and producer to his resume credits.

As the Artistic Director & Founder of 3SB Theatre Co LLC Theatre and Entertainment Production Company, based in New York, Marcos Luis may be most recognizable for His National commercials, Print campaigns, as well as appearances popular prime time TV shows. He is also the Host and Creator of the 11+ year long running LIVE Artist Showcase and open mic called OneMicNite™, in NYC which showcases premiere talent in Comedy/ Poetry/ & Music of every genre, that is a NYC staple and responsible for launching the careers of numerous Independent Artists.

Marcos is a proud and active member of SAG- AFTRA , AEA ,and Spotlight Talent UK, as a OneBigMop Artist, with a BS in Theatre Arts from Northwestern University, where He studied with famed International instructor Ann Woodworth (also B.A.D.A). His studies include comedy improv at the famed Comedy Sportz in Chicago, dance training at the world renowned Giordano Dance Center, and a Jeff Award winning show, Pecong at Victory Gardens to name a few. Currently He serves as an active screening Judge for The Miami Indie Festival (Mindie Fest ),international Independent Film Festival, and The SAG-AFTRA Awards Film Nomination Committee 2018.
